What is HydroShare?

The Consortium of Universities for the Advancement of Hydrologic Science, Inc. (CUAHSI) is an organization representing more than 100 universities and is sponsored by the National Science Foundation to provide infrastructure and services to advance the development of hydrologic science and education in the United States. The CUAHSI HydroShare is being developed as an online, collaborative environment for the sharing of hydrologic data and models. It will enable people to easily share, discover and access data and models, retrieve them to their desktop or perform analysis seamlessly in a high performance computing environment, thereby enhancing research, education and application of hydrologic knowledge.

Why use HydroShare?

Save your data online safely and share them to get feedback from other people. HydroShare will provide convenient data uploading function and save them safely online. It will also have access control ability to provide user with multiple ways of sharing their data and the corresponding metadata. Besides, the system will provide the function of commenting on the shared data and models, so as to help user get constructive feedback from other people.

Get credits of your data and models when cited by others. Each data or model uploaded in the HydroShare system will be assigned with citation information. Whatâ€™s more, in the future the system may provide the DOI for the data or models which are approved and published in the HydroShare system.

Easy access to hydrologic data and models shared by other users or third part organizations. HydroShare will provide powerful searching functions to help user find out the data and models they are looking for. These data and models will be from the other users or the third party organization such as USGS, CUAHSI HydroServer, Earth Exchange, etc.

Collaborate with other people for hydrological research. HydroShare will provide the social media functionality to enhance collaboration for hydrologic research. User will be able to create or join the groups they have interests in. And the group will provide functions for collaborations among group members to share data and models, participate in discussions, and add group blogs.

What is HydroShare's development status?

Hydroshare is currently in Phase 1 of development.

What is a resource?

In HydroShare, we define a resource as digital content which includes the science data files or model files and their corresponding metadata information. HydroShare will include multiple resource types such as time series, geographic feature class, geographic raster, multidimensional space time array data (NetCDF), model, document, etc. Currently, HydroShare beta system supports resource types of time series and geographic feature class (or Geoanalytics).

How do I create a resource?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "Create Resource" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Choose data files stored on your local computer to add to your resource. Add or delete rows as necessary using the "Add a Row" or "Delete" buttons.
  4. Select your resource type from the dropdown menu.
  5. Enter the remaining metadata for your resource (optional).
  6. Click the "Create Resource" button.

How do I view, export, edit, or delete a resource?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "My Resources" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Use the search bar to find the resource that you'd like to modify.
  4. Select "Editable by me" to filter the search results to only show resources that you have permission to edit.

From here, you can do any of the following:

  • View the resource by clicking on its name.
  • Edit the resource by viewing the resources and then clicking the blue "Edit" button at the top of the page.
  • Export the resource by viewing the resources, scrolling down, and clicking the blue "Export All" button..
  • Delete the resource by viewing the resource and then clicking the blue "Delete" button at the top of the page.

How do I comment on or rate a resource?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "My Resources" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Use the search bar to find the resource that you'd like to rate or comment on.
  4. Comment on a resource by scrolling to the bottom of the page, entering your name and email address, typing your comment, and clicking the black "Comment" button.
  5. Currently HydroShare does not support rating resources. You may, however, rate a comment by clicking the "+1" next to the comment.

What is a "group" in HydroShare?

In HydroShare, we define a group as a collection of HydroShare users who have the similar area of interests to support collaboration among them. HydroShare will enable users to share their data and models in the group, participate in discussions, and add group blogs, etc.

How do I create a group?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "Collaborate" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Create a group by clicking the green button labeled "+ Create a group" on the top left side of the page.

How do I filter or search for a group?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "Collaborate" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Filter or search for a group by typing in keywords in the text box labeled "search keywords" and click the blue "Search groups" button.

How do I join or comment on a group?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "Collaborate" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Find the group you would like to join and/or comment on using the search method outlined above.
  4. Join or comment on a group by selecting a group from your search results.

How do I edit or delete a group?

  1. Sign in to HydroShare.
  2. Click on "Collaborate" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Select "Groups I own" under the "Show" menu on the left. Search may used to filter further if necessary.
  4. Edit or delete a group by selecting one of your groups from these search results.
