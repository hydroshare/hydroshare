Collaborate

GROUPS
Groups allow research teams to share Private resources among members. These are often themed around a concept or event. Access and permissions to Resources are administered by designated individuals in each Group.

COMMUNITIES
Communities are designed for Groups to share resources more seamlessly, fostering public data sharing and open access. Current examples of Communities include collaborations between large research networks and collections of universities.
